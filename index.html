About Me

My name is Muhammad Imron. I was born on June 30, 2000, in Magetan. I am the second of three siblings. My older sister is named Aini Nurjanah, and my younger brother is named Rizki Nur Rohmat. I spent my childhood in Magetan, East Java, with my parents.

I continued my higher education at Pelita Bangsa University, majoring in Computer Engineering. During my college years, I actively participated in extracurricular badminton activities every week. In addition to studying and sports, I also took on some part-time jobs. One of the work experiences I had was as a Quality Control operator at an automotive company in Cikarang. Here is a brief biography of a Muhammad Imron with all his simplicity. Hopefully, it can inspire anyone to be themselves.

My Projects

PT Futaba Industrial Indonesia is a manufacturing company specializing in the production of automotive components, particularly for four-wheeled vehicles. PT Futaba Industrial Indonesia is a Japanese Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) company established in December 2011 and is under the umbrella of Futaba Industrial Co., Ltd. The company has a factory located in the GIIC Industrial Area, Bekasi. Its main products include Exhaust Manifolds and Body Parts.

PT Cabinindo Putra was established in 1991 and operates in the Bekasi area. Our primary specialization lies in Aluminum Die Casting and Plastic Injection for two-wheel and four-wheel motor vehicle components, as well as electronic components. Mass production at PT Cabinindo Putra began in 1991. Thanks to effective management systems, since 2007 PT Cabinindo Putra has held ISO 9001 and 14001 certifications.
